Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Pawssible (“the App”, “we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, stores, and deletes information when you connect your TikTok account and use the App.

Information we collect

  • TikTok account authorization data, such as OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens.
  • Basic account identifiers needed to associate a connected TikTok account with the App.
  • Video upload job details, such as local file path references, upload status, and timestamps.
  • Caption, hashtag, and draft metadata submitted for upload.
  • Webhook or status callback data returned by TikTok relating to upload processing.

How we use information

  • To authenticate your TikTok account connection.
  • To upload authorized video content as drafts or private-safe content to your TikTok account.
  • To monitor upload status and report success or failure.
  • To troubleshoot errors and maintain the App.

How we store information

Tokens and related upload metadata are stored server-side or on infrastructure controlled by the App operator. We do not intentionally expose your tokens publicly. Access is limited to what is necessary to operate the App.

How long we retain information

We retain tokens and upload records only as long as needed to operate the connected upload workflow, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only with service providers or platforms necessary to operate the App, including TikTok, hosting providers, and secure infrastructure providers.

Your choices

  • You may revoke the App’s access through your TikTok account settings where supported.
  • You may request deletion of stored account connection data by contacting us.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect stored tokens and related metadata, but no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

Children

The App is not intended for children under the minimum age required to use TikTok or applicable digital services.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
